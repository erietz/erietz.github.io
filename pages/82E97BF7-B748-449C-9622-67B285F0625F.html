Beverly Dingus Instructions

Three Rules

  1. Do not expose her to light.
  2. Do not get her wet.
  3. Do NOT feed her after midnight!

Food

  • She eats about \(\frac{1}{2} \text{cup}\) of dog food total per day.
    • The scoop size in the dog food bag is \(\frac{1}{4} \text{cup}\).
    • She eats three times per day.
    • So fill the scoop about \(\frac{2}{3}\) full each time.
    • \(3 \times \frac{1}{4} \times \frac{2}{3} = 3 \times \frac{2}{12} = \frac{6}{12} = \frac{1}{2}\) ðŸ˜Š
  • She also drinks water (\(H_2O\)) out of her bowl.

Daily Routine

  • We usually get her out of her long term confinement area (LTCA) around 7am.
    • Make sure she pees in her litter box before leaving or she will pee (probably on a bathroom mat).
    • Play with her for awhile
    • She likes when you roll balls
  • When you have to go to work put her back in the bedroom with the door closed
    • She does not need locked in the LTCA.
    • She can play around in the room.
    • Make sure to open the blinds so she has some light.
  • If you get the chance repeat play time at lunch.
    • If its nice out I usually take her to the dog park to play.
      • Put her purple harness on and the leash on to get her to the dog park.
      • She will put up a fight putting the harness on so you kind of have to chase her down and give her treats.
      • If there are no other dogs in the park dogs you can take the leash off.
  • She should go back into the bedroom with the door closed after lunch if you leave.
  • If you are at home all day she can come out or stay in the room.
    • Your choice depending on level of annoyance.
  • We usually take her out and leave her out after 5pm.
  • We put her back in the LTCA (locked up) around 10-10:30pm for bed.
    • Make sure the blinds are closed for warmth.

Other

  • Dog treats are in the console table behind the couch
  • A bully stick will keep her entertained for a long time
    • She likes the esophagus ones but they are stinky
  • The pupperoni and beef sticks are way too big and she will choke so you gotta cut them up into teacup sizes
  • Do not let her eat the plants. She likes to mess with them
  • If you let her on the couch, watch her closely because she will try to jump off and break her legs.
